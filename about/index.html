5 Equal-Width Columns

A layout with 3 major breakpoints that eventually results in five equally-distributed columns. On medium screens it becomes a two-column layout. Once more space becomes available the layout breaks into three columns. Finally, the layout displays all five columns side by side on screens that can accommodate them.
